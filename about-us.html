Founded in 2023 by like-minded individuals, we at Prism believe that theatre is for everyone.

With this in mind we aim to put out quality productions of lesser known shows, and to share our resources with individuals/ groups who may need it. Community is important to us and we want to pay it forward!

We believe that diversity and inclusivity is key. Prism values accessibility and we want to make our shows as accessible as possible, for our members and our audience.

We look forward to welcoming you to a production soon!
